Blow-Molded Hard Cases

Our blow-molded plastic cases provide an even more durable solution to your hard case needs. These cases are molded from high density plastic and are virtually scratch resistant. The lids and backs are double wall construction, giving them added protection. Our standard cases come in black only, but color options are available when minimum order requirements are met. Further flexibility in storing the contents of your case is gained through the use of pick and pluck foam or custom foam inserts.
