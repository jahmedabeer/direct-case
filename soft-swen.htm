Custom Soft Cases

From duffel and messenger bags to backpacks, Direct Case offers customized soft cases for just about any purpose imaginable — athletic, military, medical, etc., and are perfect due to their lightweight and collapsable features. Fabric patterns and case engineering are easily altered, allowing for easier and quicker customization.

No matter what the specific use is, cases need to be able to withstand the daily use, the rough handling, and the passage of time that come with their various uses. To help with this, we offer a wide range of fabrics, finishes and features to suit any requirements and/or environments specific to our customers needs.
