Custom Foam

Our aluminum cases are practically indestructible, making them perfect for the most punishing environments. Most commonly used for carrying pistols, rifles, photography equipment, instruments and other sensitive equipment, our aluminum cases will take the abuse while protecting your equipment stowed safely inside.With our over 20 years of experience designing cases, we know what is needed to properly protect your equipment not just on the outside of your case, but the inside as well. Our teams are able to design customized foam inserts that will allow you to both display and protect the contents of your case.

We offer several options for our foam inserts — hot melt or heat bonding, PSA adhesive and lamination, and spray adhesives. There are also a variety of colors, grades, thicknesses and textures to choose from that will both protect and compliment your equipment.

If you are not quite sure what your needs may be, our experienced sales staff will be glad to make recommendations based on the type of equipment, case, application and environment.

For help in answering any questions you may have on our foam products, or if you would like to request a catalog or samples please contact us by email or give us a call at 580-230-8606.
