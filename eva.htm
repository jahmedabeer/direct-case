Custom EVA Cases

Direct Case now offers the latest alternative to soft and hard cases – Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA). Our EVA cases feature a unique, molded foam interior, offering you a lightweight solution that is sturdy enough to protect the contents inside while providing you with a great looking case on the outside.

Our EVA cases are the perfect solution for protecting everyday items such as PDAs, eye glassess, compact discs, cellular phones, and laptops.
