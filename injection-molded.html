Injection Molded Hard Cases

Our injection-molded plastic cases provide you with an excellent lightweight and cost-effective solution to your hard case needs.

With built-in handles to provide a large gripping area for easy carrying and large, sliding closures which lock the lid into place and keep it closed, these cases are durable enough to protect sensitive equipment against the wear and tear of daily use. And all of our stock cases are able to be customized in various ways, including custom foam inserts, graphics and colors.
