Aluminum Cases

Our aluminum cases are practically indestructible, making them perfect for the most punishing environments. Most commonly used for carrying pistols, rifles, photography equipment, instruments and other sensitive equipment, our aluminum cases will take the abuse while protecting your equipment stowed safely inside.

Heavy-duty handles, latches and hinges help to make our aluminum cases a reliable and sturdy method of protecting and transporting your equipment. Additionally, thick, custom foam inserts may be added to provide further protection from shock and movement.
